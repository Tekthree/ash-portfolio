About

Trained to see

An interior architecture designer based in Seattle, graduating in 2026. Ash works across residential and commercial spaces, with a focus on how rooms actually get used: where the light lands, where people gather, where things go.

Her approach is classic with an edge. Clean lines, honest materials, and a few moments that make a space feel like it could only belong to you.
